Flexible Allowances & Exemption Limits (Old Regime)

The flexible allowances in this section are capped at their respective maximum exemption limits. This calculator automatically allocates as much as possible within those limits based on your salary. When the salary is not high enough to fund all allowances, some of them are reduced to zero and any remaining amount stays in Special Allowance (fully taxable).

• Meal allowance (annual) – Max exemption is INR 36,000.

• Communication allowance (annual) – Max exemption is INR 24,000.

• Periodicals & journals allowance (annual) – Max exemption is INR 24,000.

• Professional development allowance (annual) – Max exemption is INR 24,000.

• Leave travel allowance (annual) – Max annual entitlement is one month of Basic Salary.

• Children education allowance (annual) – Max exemption is INR 2,400.

• Car running and maintenance (annual) – Max exemption is INR 24,000.

• Driver salary (annual) – Max exemption is INR 10,800.

Note: Amounts above these limits will be taxable.
